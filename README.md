# Export Customs Declaration Tool for China Exporters

**ez9810.com** — An online tool designed for small and medium-sized Chinese export enterprises to generate customs declaration documents quickly and accurately.

---

## The Problem

Chinese exporters — especially small and medium-sized businesses — face a repetitive and error-prone process every time they ship goods:

- The same shipper information, consignee details, and product data must be manually re-entered across multiple documents: the customs draft, commercial invoice, and packing list.
- A single inconsistency between documents can lead to customs rejection, shipment delays, or complications with export tax refunds.
- Many companies lack dedicated documentation staff, leaving the burden on sales or operations teams unfamiliar with customs requirements.
- This problem is especially common in export modes such as general trade (0110) and cross-border e-commerce overseas warehouse (9810).

---

## The Solution

**ez9810.com** solves this by centralizing shipper profiles, consignee profiles, and product libraries — so exporters only enter the information once and reuse it indefinitely.

### Key Features

- **Shipper Profile Management** — Save company name, address, and contact details. Reuse across all shipments.
- **Consignee Profile Management** — Store multiple consignee records with electronic seal support.
- **Product Library** — Maintain a database of product names, HS codes, unit prices, and declaration elements.
- **One-Click Document Generation** — Select shipper, consignee, and products to automatically generate:
  - Customs Declaration Draft (报关草单)
  - Commercial Invoice (商业发票)
  - Packing List (装箱单)
- **PDF Export** — Download print-ready documents to submit to customs brokers or filing systems.
- **Electronic Seal Support** — Upload and preview company seals with simulated stamp placement before generating final documents.
